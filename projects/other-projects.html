The following projects are smaller projects that were still a lot of fun to do.

Plasma cut fish post-processed with the sand-blaster. I tried painting the surface with a blue gradient effect.

Laser cut box with many compartments. Getting the tolerances correct for a perfect snap-fit was difficult, and not quite successful. Regardless, this box was a great learning experience in laser-cutting and careful dimensioning.

Skull printed using a Makerbot (file pulled from Thingiverse). I'm surprised it came out as well as it did, considering the intricate design and the lack of support used!
