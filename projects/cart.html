--- layout: project-post title: Shopping Cart Modeling and Simulation ---

Background

The shopping cart was created for a final modeling and simulation project. My group was tasked with modeling an object from scratch and simulating common loading conditions on the object.

The dimensions for the shopping cart were based off of Patent US D530478S and modified for our needs. All modeling and simulations were done using Solidworks.

Description

I was responsible for creating drawings and models for the bottom portion of the cart (including the bottom platform and wheel supports).

As a group, we simulated three diffferent loading conditions on the cart:

  • Uniform downward force of 75 lb on basket(weight of heavy groceries)
  • Uniform downward force on both basket and bottom platform
  • Uniform downward force on basket, with one unsupported wheel (worst case condition, representing a cart with a wheel dangling above a ditch)

We created graphs of the von Mises equivalent stress and displacement for the cart under each condition, and located areas of maximum stress or displacement. We analyzed whether any condition would cause material failure, and reviewed possible design changes to improve the cart (adding additional fillets, reinforcing the wheel axel support, etc.).
